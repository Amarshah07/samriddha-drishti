Empowering the Visually Impaired with AI Technology

Samriddha Drishti helps individuals with visual impairments by providing real-time object detection, text reading, and navigation assistance using smart AI-powered glasses.

Key Features

Object Detection

Detect objects in the environment in real-time, enabling visually impaired users to interact with their surroundings.

Face Recognition

Recognize familiar faces to help users interact confidently in social settings.

Text Reading

Read printed text (OCR) aloud, allowing users to understand documents, menus, and signs independently.

QR Code Detection

Scan QR codes for additional information, enhancing the accessibility of digital content.

How It Works

Samriddha Drishti uses advanced computer vision and AI models like YOLOv5 and TensorFlow Lite to process visual information and provide real-time feedback through voice guidance. The device is lightweight, affordable, and designed for ease of use in everyday life.
