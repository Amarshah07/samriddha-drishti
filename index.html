About the Project

Samriddha Drishti is an AI-powered assistive ecosystem combining smart glasses and a smart blind stick prototype. Together, they provide scene understanding, navigation support, obstacle detection, and real-time audio feedback for the visually impaired.

How It Works

1. Wear the smart glasses with built-in camera, microphone and speaker.
2. Carry the smart blind stick equipped with ultrasonic sensors and vibration alerts.
3.Use simple voice commands like ‚ÄúDescribe‚Äù, ‚ÄúRead text‚Äù, or ‚ÄúFind bottle‚Äù.
4.Glasses provide scene description, face/QR recognition, and text reading via audio.

6.Together, they enable the user to navigate confidently, read independently, and interact naturally with the world.
